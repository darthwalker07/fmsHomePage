ABOUT NCHHD

  • NCHHD (National Council For Handloom & Handicraft Development) was established in the year 1983-84 under the aegis of Govt. of India, Ministry of Textiles, Office of Development Commissioner (Handicrafts).
  • We are an apex body of 30 State /Central Govt. Handicrafts / Handlooms Development Corporations with its well-knit members throughout the country.
  • NCHHD works on artisan clusters under the Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries (SFURTI) in a major way, acting as the Nodal Agency with the support of its resources, outreach, experience, and planning.

About NCHHD FMS & MIS

The FMS and MIS 2.0 is a digital platform that simplifies the management of cluster funds for IAs working under NCHHD, featuring various tools such as email support, reporting, and a Management Information System for comprehensive monitoring and decision-making.
Its flexible design allows for future adaptability and scalability.
